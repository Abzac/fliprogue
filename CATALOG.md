# FlipRogue

FlipRogue is a tiny roguelike RPG for Flipper Zero.

Explore pocket-sized ASCII dungeons, choose a class, gather gold and charms, brave fire, water, ice, traps, grates, chests, mimics, lurkers, eels, dragonlings, and the Yonder Warden.

The game has 18 floors, procedural rooms, compact tile sprites, persistent high scores, unknown magic items, class perks, and short dangerous runs built for the tiny screen.

Inspired by Brogue, NetHack, Pathos, and Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.
